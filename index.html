Jesús Arroyo

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Maryland, College Park, and an Adjunct Assistant Research Scientist in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics at Johns Hopkins University. I work with Professor Vince Lyzinski at UMD, and with Professors Carey E. Priebe and Joshua T. Vogelstein at JHU.

My research interests include statistical network analysis, machine learning, high dimensional data analysis, and applications to neuroimaging.

Previously, I completed my PhD in the Department of Statistics at the University of Michigan, advised by Professor Liza Levina. I received a B.Sc. in Applied Mathematics and a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering from the Mexico Autonomous Institute of Technology (ITAM).
